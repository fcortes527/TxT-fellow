Cool Math Site

This video is about the famous serires: 1-1+1-1+1-1+1... It's interesting how a series could be logically solved to three different sollutions. Even more interesting is that a series consisting of only 1 or 0 for any n, could have a sum that is neither! However, for the sum of this series to be 1/2 makes sence in a way because it is exactly the average of the other two solutions: 0 and 1.
